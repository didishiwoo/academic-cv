abstract: "This study characterizes the broadband dielectric properties of 127 fresh ex vivo thyroid tissue samples in the 100-4000 MHz range using the coaxial probe method. Subgroup analysis was performed according to AJCC TNM staging criteria to explore the correlation between dielectric properties and tumor stage. Cole-Cole model fitting and Wilcoxon rank-sum tests were employed for statistical analysis. The results demonstrate that relative permittivity can significantly distinguish malignant from normal tissues (p < 0.001), offering a potential new biomarker for thyroid cancer staging."
